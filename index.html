Lambanog

A clear-colored alcoholic drink derived from coconut-palm juices produced only in the Philippine Islands.

Infanta™ Lambanog

The very first, world-class brand of Lambanog. The official alcohol of the Philippines. Classed under its own; it is a type of distilled spirit among the likes of tequila, vodka, rum, soju and sake.

Lambanog is a distilled spirit of forty percent Alc/Vol (80 proof).

From Treetop to Bottle

The process begins with the palm tree. Gatherers climb trees to prune coconut flowers so that the sap drips into bamboo vessels. Fermentation turns the sap into toddy similar to wine. The toddy is then distilled and filtered to produce lambanog.
